Louis Patridge

Louis Patrick James Partridge (born 3 June 2003) is an English actor of film and television. He is best known for his roles as Lord Tewkesbury in the Netflix films Enola Holmes (2020) and Enola Holmes 2 (2022),[2] and Sid Vicious in the FX and Star miniseries Pistol (2022).[3] Early life Partridge was born on 3 June 2003 in Wandsworth, London. He is of Welsh descent on his mother's side.[1] He has an older and a younger sister.[3] Partridge attended Dulwich Preparatory School. He played rugby with Battersea Ironsides Sports Club.[4] He then went to secondary school at Alleyn's School, where he was a member of Tyson's House. He completed his A Levels in 2021 with A*s in French and English, and an A in Film Studies.[5].
